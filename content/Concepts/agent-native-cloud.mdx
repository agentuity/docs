## What Is an Agent-Native Cloud?

**Agent-Native** means the cloud itself is designed for AI agents as the *primary* citizens â€” not retro-fitted for them. Instead of asking agents to squeeze into workflows that were built for humans or stateless micro-services, an agent-native cloud gives them native abilities to collaborate, reason, persist state, and evolve.

In short, an agent-native platform is to AI agents what *cloud-native* was to containers: the right environment, abstractions, and tooling built in from day one.
